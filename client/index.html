//About our app

Having a good start in your career is a phenomenal booster, therefore getting the right internship experiences is key.

It is fair to say that the rule of 3 rounds applies, every summer during Bachelor's degree studies students can experiment with an internship. Which one? Are you ready for 3 misfires? :)

Nowadays, we have a lot of internship opportunities, every new year new options get revealed. Reality and statistics say that we, as students, know so little about those and how those can contribute to our growth goal. For example, create an API endpoint in GO to be used in production and to brag about it to my friends. :)

//Our goal

Making the proccess of finding an internship easier!

Intermittent interactions between the students looking for internships and the companies offering those internship opportunities. The structured long term interaction channels to foster a genuine relationship are missing.

A continuously living student profile matched against internship market openings based on current skill set, learning goals, benefits, availability and other criteria.
